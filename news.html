What's New?

New Command: $player [name/id] [PlayerName/PlayerID]

$player allows you to search for a player given their ID or their name! In addition to information about the player's
current loadout, you'll also be able to see what their support summons are and their star character.

New Command: $crew [name/id] [CrewName/CrewID]

$crew allows you to search for a crew given their ID or their name! You'll be able to see the crew's rank in the last
Unite and Fight (guild war) as well as their name history.

New Commands: $bugreport and $feedback

These commands will allow you to report bugs to me or give me some feedback!


For information on how to use any of these commands, please see the command list.

Changes and Improvements

$Spark
  • You can now set all your spark resources with one command! See the command list for more information.
    • Thanks to Alvin for suggesting this.
  • $spark add/subtract/set now tells you how many total draws you have after adding to/subtracting from/setting your spark resources.
  • If you have over 100% progress towards a spark, the portion of the progress bar that would overflow is now layered on top and
    colored red instead (just like GBF characters who have 200% charge bar limit).
    • Thanks to Dopemeister for suggesting this.
  • Added support for Japanese characters, as well as many other characters to be displayed in the spark profile.
    • Thanks to マムクート for bringing this up to me.
